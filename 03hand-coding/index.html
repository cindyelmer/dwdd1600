My Story

I began Flowerchild Floral as a passion project in 2021. After taking a beginner floral design course with Andrea Seehagen, the owner of the renowned luxury wedding florist company Delaflor, I quickly fell in love with the craft. There's something deeply therapeutic about working with fresh blooms to create stunning arrangements—it's a form of soul healing for me. Even more rewarding is the opportunity to help people celebrate life's most meaningful moments by designing flowers that become part of their cherished memories.

Why Us?

At Flowerchild Floral, we are proud to be a locally owned and operated business with over three years of experience creating custom floral designs for life's most important moments. From bridal bouquets that reflect your love story to elegant arrangements for school dances and thoughtful funeral sprays, our mission is to help you celebrate, honor, and cherish what matters most. We specialize in crafting one-of-a-kind arrangements that perfectly capture the emotions and beauty of your event, offering personalized consultations and attention to detail every step of the way. Whether you're planning an intimate wedding, a milestone celebration, or a heartfelt tribute, we're here to bring your vision to life with fresh, locally sourced flowers from Red Mountain. Let us be a part of your special occasion, and together, we'll create memories that last a lifetime.
